Fire Safety Expert System Full Antigravity Prompt

Build a SWI-Prolog Expert System with XPCE GUI.

Key requirements:
- Dynamic knowledge base using :- dynamic building/9.
- Building(Name,Type,Floors,Occupancy,Exits,Staircases,Alarm,Sprinkler,Lighting).
- Building types: office, hotel, school, hospital, factory, apartment.
- Use assertz/1, retractall/1, findall/3, member/2, fail/0, if-then-else.
- Infer required exits, staircases, violations, recommendations, score, risk, compliance.

Recommendations mapped to violations. Hospitals always require sprinklers. Factories require alarm and sprinklers. Hotels over 150 occupants require at least 3 exits. Schools over 200 occupants require at least 2 staircases.

GUI buttons: Add Building, View Buildings, Assess Building, Delete Building, View Guidelines, View Example Buildings, Exit.

Assessment report must show: building details, occupancy category, building risk category, safety score, risk level, compliance status, mandatory issues, recommendations.
